Our Mission

Founded in 2022 by a team of robotics engineers and AI researchers from MIT and Stanford, Quantum is on a mission to bridge the gap between humans and machines through advanced humanoid robotics.

We believe that humanoid robots should enhance human capabilities, not replace them. Our robots are designed to work alongside humans, taking on dangerous, repetitive, or physically demanding tasks while maintaining the highest standards of safety and ethical considerations.
